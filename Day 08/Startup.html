Support

Common questions.

How do I unlock a scooter?
Open the Zipp app, tap "Find a Ride", point your camera at the QR code on any scooter nearby, and you're good to go. The scooter unlocks in under 3 seconds.
Where can I park after my ride?
Park in any designated Zipp zone shown on the map — usually near footpaths, cycle stands, or marked parking spots. Parking outside a zone incurs a ₹50 fee. Never block footpaths or ramps.
Can I pause my ride mid-way?
Yes. Tap "Pause Ride" in the app to lock the scooter temporarily and keep it reserved for you. A ₹1/min pause fee applies to keep the scooter held in place for up to 10 minutes.
How do I cancel my Monthly Pass subscription?
Go to Account → Subscription → Cancel Plan in the app. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. No fees, no questions asked.
What if a scooter has low battery or a fault?
The app shows battery level before you unlock. If you encounter a fault mid-ride, tap "Report Issue" — you won't be charged for that session and our team will collect the scooter within the hour.
